Biography

Zitu Kundu is a graduate researcher at the Department of Geology & Mining, University of Barishal, Bangladesh. His MS thesis — currently defended — is titled "A Comprehensive Geochemical Investigation of the Kuakata Beach Sand: Implications for Provenance, Weathering, Heavy Metal Contamination, and Natural Radioactivity."

His research works with major oxides, trace elements, and rare earth elements (REEs) to constrain sediment provenance, weathering history, and geochemical conditions of the Bengal coastal system. He also conducts radiological risk assessment, health risk assessment, and spatial distribution analysis of heavy metals and NORMs.

Zitu has 2 published papers in Elsevier journals, 1 manuscript under review at a Q1 journal (Marine Pollution Bulletin), and 4 manuscripts in preparation.
